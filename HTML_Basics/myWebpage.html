Brandon Sanderson

Who is Brandon Sanderson?

Brandon Sanderson is an author from American Fork, Utah, USA. The first book he ever published was "Elantris" and it was published in 2005. After that, was Mistborn: The Final Empire, which was published in 2006, with the first trilogy being finished in 2008. The Way of Kings was started in the late 1990s, but finished and published in 2010. Brandon has three kids and a wife, Emily, who is the head of their company, Dragonsteel.

The Stormlight Archive

Brandon has writen many books, but his most famous and most reconizable series is The Stormlight Archive. The Stormlight Archive follows many main protagonists; the main one being Kaladin Stormblessed. The Stormlight Archive's magic system revolves around semi-other-worldy creatures known as inteligent spren. These spren can form bonds with humans and give them special powers that can range from making illutions, playing with the direction of gravity, turning something into dust, forming bonds with other people and things, making stone liquid, making stuff slick or not slick to play with movment, and healing wounds. Brandon has plans to make this seires ten books long. So far, five are released so we are half way there!

Mistborn Era One

Mistborn Era One is a trilogy set in broken wasteland planet. If we were to compare the technology for them to us, it is around the renassiance. This world's magic system involves metel. Yes, as in the metel a spoon in made of. Some people are born with the ability to "burn" a metal, which means they can ingest some of a metal they are able to burn and like, activate it in their body and is gives powers. Generally, people can only burn one metal. These people are called Mistings. However, some people can burn all of them and these people are known as Mistborns. They burn metals through Allomancy. That is where people directly ingest metals to get the power. There are other ways to burn metals like Feruchemy. Feruchemy is where people can store power or life or strength into metal that coresponds with the abiltiy being stored, but the metal has to in direct contact to the person's skin. There is also a third way called Hemalurgy. This is where a type of metal is physicly driven through someone who can burn that metal and the power is taken out of the person and into the spike. Tis spike can then be driven into someone else and the power is transfered to them. This is the only way for someone to burn more than one metal. From what I remeber about this, someone cannot wield Allomancy and Feruchemy at the same time.
